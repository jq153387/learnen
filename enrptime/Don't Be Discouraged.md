#Lesson 78 Don't Be Discouraged

###Dialogue

Teacher: Your written English is **fairly** good,but your spoken English needs **improvement**.

Student: I know, but speaking English is difficult.

Teacher: Don't be **discouraged**. **Practice** makes perfect.

---
**discouraged** [dis'kʌridʒd] 灰心；(v.)勸阻（discourage的過去分詞），使失去勇氣；(adj.)氣餒的   
Not discouraged , Johnson decided to build a ' new ' line across the Superior Avenue viaduct to Public Square.

**improvement** [ɪm'prʊvmənt] 起色；(n.)改進，提高   
There is so much room for improvement with our sport, education, healthcare and emergency services.

**Practice** ['præktɪs] (n.)實踐，練習，慣例      
Einstein was an exclusive theorist who showed no interest in the practice of his own thinkings and theories.

**fairly** ['fɛrli] (adv.)相當地，公平地，簡直   
What's unusual about the electronics payment suit is that it seems to be a fairly blatant case of China ignoring its WTO obligations.
