#My **Pacific** **Quest**

>Some of the most remote places on Earth are only reachable by sailboat.

**New Zealander** Ellis Emmett lives for **adventure**. He has **conquered** every mountain and river in his home country and now has his sights set on the bigger picture, but first he must get a boat. Then, he must learn to **sail** since he wants travel in his **sailboat** around the Pacific **Ocean**. In My Pacific Quest, on National Geographic, Emmett **struggles with** the **physical** and **mental** challenges of sailng, but he soon learns from friends and goes off into the deep blue sea.
